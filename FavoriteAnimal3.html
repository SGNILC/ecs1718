During the 1960'the Japanese breed of pigs that weren't big enough to be sent to western zoos stayed and were domesticated on the farmland. These types of pigs were branched under the name "Miniature pigs." Although they're born small, these bigs can grow up to wiegh 200-500 lbs. Like many other farm animal, they enjoy a diet of wheat and soybeans, along with other nuts and seeds.
